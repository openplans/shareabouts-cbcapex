What physical projects could make our community better?

Use this map to suggest where City government should spend capital budget dollars in your neighborhood. Your voice helps us to spend money on things that are important to you. Tell us what matters most to you.

To add a budget request, click "Get Started" below. Then, click on the “Identify a Need” box in the lower left corner. Drag the map until the map marker is over the location for your desired budget request, then fill out the pop-up form.

Citizens pay taxes with an expectation that tax dollars will be used to improve facilities and services in their communities. Citizens also have the right to influence how those tax dollars are spent by providing input to their local Community Boards. Public input is necessary to ensure that new needs are identified and requests are justified.

Every year, each Community Board establishes and submits a prioritized list of capital budget requests for physical projects throughout their district. The Mayor, City Council, and Borough President review these requests and consider them when determining the City’s budget.

By using this map, you are participating in the process of identifying and justifying capital budget projects. The Community Board will use the projects identified on this map to help prioritize budget requests.
